Where and What?

Portland is a place full of bars, theaters, musical venues, and amazing bikepaths that should be taken advantage of for tourists and visitors to the city!

Some of the amazing musical venus in Portland include the Moda Center and Crystal Ballroom where you can see popular performers! You can also bike along the paths by the river to end up at the Electric Bicycle Shop to rent whatever you need for traversing Portland during your stay!
